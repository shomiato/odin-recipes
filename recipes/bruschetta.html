Bruschetta

Description:

Bruscehtta, while a simple dish, is absolutely delicious. When one uses fresh ingredients there is nothing tastier.

Ingredients:

  • French Loaf
  • Olive oil
  • Diced tomatos
  • Parmesean cheese
  • Garlic
  • Black peper
  • Kosher salt
  1. Slice the bread into thin slices, and brush with olive oil.
  2. Toast bread in oven until golden brown.
  3. Mix the diced tomatoes, garlic and parmesean in bowl, then add olive oil, salt and pepper to mix.
  4. Spoon mixture onto toasted bread
