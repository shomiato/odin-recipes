Sourdough

Description:

Sourdough bread is one of the ideal forms of bread, for almost any application. From sandwiches, to toasting, to croutons, it can do it all!

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Sourdough starter
  • Olive oil
  • Bread flour
  • Sea salt
  1. Mix together all the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Cover the dough with cover, and let sit
  3. Divide and shape the dough into proper balls.
  4. Allow dough to rise a second time.
  5. Bake the dough in oven until golden brown.
