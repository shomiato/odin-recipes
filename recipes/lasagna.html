Lasagna

Description:

The lasagna is a staple of any italian household. Made with a mixture of pasta, cheese, tomato and meat, it offers a hearty, filling meal.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Meat sauce
  • Riccota Cheese
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  1. Start by making the sauce with ground beef, tomato and spices.
  2. Let the sauce simmer while the noodles boil, and the cheese is shredded.
  3. Assemble the layers of in alternating pattern of noodle, sauce, and cheese, repeat until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in oven until cheese is golden brown.
